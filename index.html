Welcome to all things Cryptidcore, dear reader.

What is Cryptidcore?

Cryptidcore is a genre or aesthetic which focuses on the concepts of the supernatural, cryptids, aliens, mystery, and adventure. It is a romanticization of a life hunting evidence of ghosts and creatures such as Bigfoot or Mothman; delving into conspiracy theories and sometimes bordering on true crime.

The aesthetic itself is heavily associated with Cryptozoology, the study of creatures from urban myths and folklore. Hence, the inherent mystery of the unknown and drive to discover are essential themes of Cryptidcore.

Those that are part– or want to be part– of the Cryptidcore community enjoy the thrill of expanding their mundane horizons, opening up to the possibility of things beyond themselves as well as experiencing anything mythical and strange.

It’s an aesthetic for those in the ground looking up in the sky, searching for a pair of round, red eyes to stare back at them.
