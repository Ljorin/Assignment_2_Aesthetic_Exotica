Visuals

When it comes to conjuring up what Cryptidcore looks like in one’s head, it’ll often involve the visuals of Ouija boards, old VHS tapes, old cameras (preferably Polaroid) and tape recorders, abandoned places (i.e a run-down asylum or haunted house), news clippings of cryptid sightings, a messy board with yarn connecting pieces of information, dark forests, anything involving aliens, and tarot cards.

Outfit of the Mystery Solver

The attire of any ghost-hunting, mystery-solving, individual often comes in muted, sometimes earthy, colours. The style leans toward a comfy, retro, casual, and vintage look. One might see apparel of graphic tees, flannel shirts, cargo pants, jean or corduroy or plaid jackets, combat boots, patches (either iron-on or sewn), large sweaters, and baseball caps or beanies with something related to cryptids or supernatural.

Activities for the Curious & Adventurous

So, dear reader, you want to delve into the world of strange and things that go bump in the night? Then you might want to consider the following: Researching local mysteries, Plan your wedding with your cryptid of choice, Be paranoid, Look for anything involving aliens or anything involving government cover ups, Look into conspiracy theories, Watch documentaries about the supernatural or conspiracy theories, and keeping a journal with notes on everything.

Here are more activities to consider:

Research urban myths and folklore

Tell scary stories around the campfire

Engage in spooky ghost hunting

Build an evidence board for your conspiracies

Thrift shop for haunted things

Look for and listen to number stations

Take polaroid photographs or record old film

Go for a relaxing walk in the woods

Explore abandoned buildings
