Depiction in Media

The larger-than-life intrigue mixed with grounded reality makes for a great opportunity to explore fantastical and surreal storytelling without having to leave the familiarity of our real world; to leave to an entirely new world with its own rules, races, and niche backstory.

Instead, Cryptidcore aims to bring the magic to our front door; to spice up the mundane lives of Planet Earth with vague whisperings of a secret and mythical world just at the corner of our eyes.

Below you’ll see some examples of how Cryptidcore has influenced media. Who knows? You might even find some interesting recommendations.
